OUR POLICY

PLEASE TAKE NOTE: All Security deposits paid towards appointments of any service provided by Thee Cl Experience are non-refundable (Refunds will only be given at the discretion of Thee Cl Experience).

In order to secure an Appointment, a deposit is required. NO DEPOSIT means NO APPOINTMENT and appointments are given on a first come first serve basis. Should you be unable to attend your appointment and need to cancel or reschedule, please give us 24hr notice. We advise that you show up in time for your appointment or else your appointment will be rescheduled to the next available date. A 20min Grace - period is given to clients if any lateness is to occur, any arrival after 20 min grace period will result in rescheduling. Clients Rescheduling with less than 24hr notice will be required to pay for their appointments in Full before choosing the next available slot, failure to that will result in total loss of the deposit and may be required to pay a new deposit to secure another appointment.Lastly, please come alone for your appointment, No Guests and No Children.
